Research and Publications

  • Forensic Analysis of SD Card and Flight Log Artifacts of DJI Phantom 4 Pro V2.0

    Published In: Information and Communication Technology for Competitive Strategies (ICTCS) 2025.
    This paper performs a forensic assessment of the DJI Phantom 4 Pro V2.0, the most common consumer drone type, based on evidence sets derived from the NIST CFReDS. Evidence was extracted from internal data storage media, removable SD cards, as well as mobile app artifacts with a focus on the DAT telemetry log files, PARM configuration files, USER mission briefs, as well as EXIF data sets on file media. Autopsy, DatCon, as well as ExifTool tools were used in log parsing, reconstructing flight paths, as well as proving EXIF data integrity.
